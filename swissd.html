swissd.jsx

for After Effects

Swissd is an After Effects script for creating fixed width text layouts.

{% capture mddisloc %} During a workshop I gave called ["Typography And Automation"]() Julian a.k.a. [@natael](https://github.com/natael) came up with the idea of creating fixed width layouts in InDesign. His result, also called [swissd](http://fabiantheblind.github.io/Typography-And-Automation/natael.html), was so interesting I took it on to create something similar for After Effects. I did not port his code. I wrote my own. It is published on [aescipts.com](http://aescripts.com/swissd) and open source under MIT license. {% endcapture %} {{ mddisloc | markdownify }}
