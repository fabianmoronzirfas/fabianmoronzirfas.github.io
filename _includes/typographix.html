Programming Typography

During my studies I became a member of the group Grenzfarben. Together with Ina Dautier, Antje Stürholz, Tanja Sannewald, Sebastian Matthäus, Steven Nikolic, Oliver Otto, Peter Sekan and I produced, since 2008, several product catalouges for JustMusic and Sound Service. We started out working in Quark Express and had to format every paragraph of these catalouges, that have more than 300 pages and 5000 to 7000 products, by hand. When we made the switch to InDesign I was tired of clicking. So I tried to automate the process with JavaScript/ExtendScript. It worked. Now we have a semiautomatic process that gives us more time for design and I never stoped writing scripts. Below you will find Swissd.jsx and Monoline Text.jsx for After Effects together with a workshop I gave at the FHPotsdam about Typography and Automation and a semiautomatic process that imports my project data from Incom.org into a Layout.
