Geographic Visualization

While studying at the FH Potsdam I took interest in plotting data onto interactive maps. For a project in the field of "Design of Software Interfaces" Felix Lange, Sabine Richter, Peter Sekan and I developed a concept and wrote a mockup for a desaster management software on the maeve table. This was the starting point for understanding maps and location data. Based on this knowledge I developed Locations.jsx and AEMap.jsx for Adobe After Effects that allow the creation of location based data and developed with Peter Sekan UE Lärm. That started as a geographic project but became a a data visualization.
