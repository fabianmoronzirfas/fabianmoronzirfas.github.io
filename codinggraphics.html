Geographic Visualization

Locations
AEMap
Tangible Maps

While studying at the FH Potsdam I took interest in plotting data onto interactive maps. For a project in the field of "Design of Software Interfaces" I have been part of a team that developed a concept and wrote a mockup for a desaster management software for the maeve table. This was the starting point for understanding maps and location data. Based on this knowledge I developed Locations.jsx and AEMap.jsx for Adobe After Effects that allow the creation animations with location based data.

Typography and Automation
Incom.org 2 Layout

During my studies I became a member of the group Grenzfarben. Together with them I produced, since 2008, several product catalouges for JustMusic and Sound Service. We started out working in Quark Express and had to format every paragraph of these catalouges, that have more than 300 pages and 5000 to 7000 products, by hand. When we made the switch to InDesign I was tired of clicking. So I tried to automate the process with JavaScript/ExtendScript. It worked. Now we have a semiautomatic process that gives us more time for design and I never stoped writing scripts. Below you will find Swissd.jsx and Monoline Text.jsx for After Effects together with a workshop I gave at the FHPotsdam about Typography and Automation and a semiautomatic process that imports my project data from Incom.org into a Layout.
