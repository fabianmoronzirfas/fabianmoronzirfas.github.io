AEMap.jsx

for After Effects

creates equirectangular maps with 287 separate masks on solids or shape layers grouped in jurisdictions.

{% capture mddismap %} While preparing for ma B. A. thesis I found a [GEOJson](http://www.geojson.org/) public domain [data set](https://github.com/johan/world.geo.json) on github that contains country boundaries. I first wrote a [InDesign version](https://github.com/fabiantheblind/IDJSMap) for that and ported it afterwards to After Effects. Than I created the interface, a demo video and the tutorial and published that on github.com. After three days I got an email by [Lloyd Alvarez](http://lloydalvarez.com/), the founder of [aescripts.com](http://aescripts.com), if I would like to publish my script on his site. I felt flattered. See the project below or [download the script](http://aescripts.com/aemap/) and examine the code. This script is open source and under MIT license. {% endcapture %} {{ mddismap | markdownify }}
