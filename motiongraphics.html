{% capture modesc %} #Hello, you reached my motion graphics overview. As I mentioned before, already as a child I loved cartoons and animations. Around 2008 I started working with forseesense](http://forseesense.com/) on several projects. While working on these projects I always came across problems that had to be solved by scripting. Most of the time these scripts are tiny helper tools that don't make sense to others but some of them are more. They turned into my open source scripts provided on [aescripts.com](http://aescripts.com/). For these scripts I created demos that you can consider advertising for what people can do with them. Besides these videos I created several characters and infographic animations for exhibitions/science centers. See my favorite moving pictures below. {% endcapture %} {{ modesc | markdownify }}
