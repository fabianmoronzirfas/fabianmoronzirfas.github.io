{% capture modesc %} #Hello, you reached my motion graphics overview. As I mentioned before already as a child I loved cartoons and animations. Around 2008 I started working with [forseesense](http://forseesense.com/) on several projects. While working on these projects I always came across problems that had to be solved by scripting. Most of the time these scripts are tiny helper tools that don't make sense to others but some of them are more. They turned into my open source scripts provided on [aescripts.com](http://aescripts.com/). For these scripts I created demos that you can consider advertising for what they can do. Besides these videos I created several character and info graphic animations for exhibitions/science centers like the [Klimahaus Bremerhaven 8° Ost](http://klimahaus-bremerhaven.de/en/), [Welios® - OÖ Science Center Wels](http://www.welios.at/en/startseite.html) the [Experimenta Heilbronn](http://www.experimenta-heilbronn.de/) and others. Enough of that. See my favorite moving pictures below. {% endcapture %} {{ modesc | markdownify }}
